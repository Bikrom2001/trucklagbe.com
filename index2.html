Truck Category

1 Ton Truck

The smallest truck on our fleet, ready to serve your minimal requirements. Some famous brands are Tata, Mahindra, Toyota etc.

2 Ton Truck

The rear body of this type of truck is 12 feet. Usually used for transporting medium type business goods and for family relocation. Popular truck brands in this category are Jac and T-King.

3.5 Ton Truck

Medium capacity. Used for small or medium goods transportation. We have some famous brands like Eicher, Mitsubishi, Toyota, Mahindra, Tata under this category.

7.5 Ton Truck

From here starts the large truck categories. Can carry up to 7 tons of goods. Some famous brands are Tata, Isuzu etc.

15 Ton Truck

Used for large shipments, these giants have the capacity of 15 tons. Under this category, we have Eicher, Tata, Ashok Layland etc. These types of trucks are 20 feet in length. Available as both open and covered van.

25 Ton Truck

Five times larger than a 5 ton truck. This monster is capable of transporting industrial grade heavy transposition. Some famous brands are Tata, Ashok Layland etc.

Prime mover/Trailer

As the name indicates, it's a prime mover! Famous brands in this category include DAF, Tata, Eicher etc. These trucks are used to transport goods in large containers. They are 40 feet in length.
